Community

Cartix is here to stay and to grow mainly through a community of open data enthusiasts driven by the spirit of
collective effort and knowledge sharing. Our main goal is to sustainably spark a movement to grow a vibrant
community of expert in data science that spans various sectors in Rwanda and beyond.

Customers

Data is continually becoming an indispensable commodity in all sectors (public, private & civil society). Cartix is built in way to cater for all the users found in those three categories. Our key added value is tailor-made dashboard. We have dashboards tailored for NGOs, MFIs, Public Institutions, etc. For any dashboard customization, it is important that it be scoped in a manner to allows for high quality, well designed and properly tested application.

blog

Welcome to Cartix blogs, a place where everyone has a story to share. Stories shared on our blog include opinions, development, achievements and challenges in the area of data science in Rwanda, the region and Africa at large. We are aware that data has no boundaries but on our platform the focus is Africa and we make sure all contributors customize their content to the African context while we think global and act local.
